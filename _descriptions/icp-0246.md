Built by Andrew Heavey and Stephen When on 29 January 1926, Andrew Heavey and Stephen J. McCrann opened the Garden Vale Cinema in premises formerly Ferriter’s Stores on Athlone's Ballymahon Road, it was the town’s second cinema. It had seating for 500 on the parterre, balcony and gallery.  

###Opening Film

The opening night audience were treated to the Colleen Moore vehicle Sally (US: First National, 1925).
